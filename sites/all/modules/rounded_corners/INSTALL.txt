Installation instructions for rounded_corners http://drupal.org/rounded_corners

1. Download rounded_corners from http://drupal.org/rounded_corners
   and extract the contents into your modules folder.
2. Check administer > settings > Rounded corners to see if jquery.corner.js
   is correctly installed.
